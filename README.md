# ArtisanArc

**ArtisanArc** is an elegant, offline-first craft supply organiser and small business toolkit. Designed for makers, artists, and independent crafters, it combines inventory tracking, project planning, visual swatches, compliance management, and sales logging — all wrapped in an ethereal UI.

---

## Features

### Core (Free Tier)
- **Inventory Manager** – Add materials (yarn, fabric, paper), assign categories, pricing, storage locations
- **QR Integration** – Generate & scan QR codes for items and reordering
- **Visual Documentation** – Attach photos, extract colors, and manage swatches
- **Craft Timeline Projects** – Track milestones and supply needs per craft
- **Compliance Tracker** – Mark safety certifications (UKCA, US equivalents)
- **Smart Shopping** – Create lists, track vendors, and compare costs
- **Multilingual** – UK and US English support
- **Offline AI Hints** – Craft-specific assistant available offline

### Business & Premium Tools
- **Daily Sales Tracker** – Log sales by day, linked to inventory
- **Revenue Summary** – Monthly views, exportable CSV/PDF reports
- **Label Templates** – Printable organization and product labels
- **Event Planning Tools** – Craft group sync with calendar integration
- **Authentication** – Biometric login + app passcode
